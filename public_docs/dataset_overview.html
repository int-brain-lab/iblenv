What is available for download ?

Collaboration-wide datasets

2025 Brain wide map data

The flagship dataset of the IBL ! See the technical paper.

2024 Reproducible ephys data

Data associated with the publication Reproducibility of in vivo electrophysiological measurements in mice.

2020 Behavioral data for standardized behaviour task

Data associated with the publication Standardized and reproducible measurement of decision-making in mice via ONE and Datajoint.

Projects

2025 Decision-making accross mouse models of Autism (Noel)

Neuropixel recordings under the IBL decision-making tasks in mice harbouring mutations of Fmr1, Cntnap2, Shank3B. The data is associated with the publication A common computational and neural anomaly across mouse models of autism.

2022 Spike sorting benchmark recordings

A list of 13 insertions in different brain regions to provide a smaller brain-wide Neuropixel benchmarks is available .

2020 Pilot neuropixels datasets

The IBL has released a handful of pilot datasets that are available for download through ONE.
