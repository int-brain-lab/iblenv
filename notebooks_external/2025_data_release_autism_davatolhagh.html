2025 - Autism Widefield

Widefield data were collected in 5 mice performing the IBL task. Animals were either wildtype, or from the autism models TRE-GCaMP6s-CAMKII; NRXN KO.

How to cite this dataset

If you are using this dataset for your research please cite Davatolhagh, M.F., Couto, J., Melin, M., Oesch, L.T., Findling, C., Morales, P., Hong, I., International Brain Laboratory, Churchland, A.K. Altered Use of Prior Expectations Shapes Behavior and Neural Dynamics in a Mouse Model of Autism in preparation.
